ITEM # 6 WAS FOUND IN VIOLATION 1 TIME(S). PUBLIC HEALTH HAZARD
Part or parts of the item were observed to be in violation which were corrected at the time of inspection.
Code Requirements: Adequate disinfection - chlorine residual maintained {7-1.4b(6)}
Inspector Findings: 1. At the time of the inspection, staff stated that the chlorinator had stopped working last night and he had been working on it this morning. The chlorinator was observed to be operating but it did not appear that any chlorine was being pulled through the tubing from the crock. Additionally, changes to the water system were not made in accordance with conditionally approved plans. A Boil Water Order is issued. The chlorinator must be repaired, changes to the water system noted elsewhere in this report approved by this Department or subsequent required changes made, and two consecutive days of satisfactory bacteriological water samples submitted to this Department before the Boil Water Order may be terminated. The Boil Water Order may not be terminated until written authorization is obtained from a representative of this Department.
